Back-to-School Checklist

Are you Ready For School?, August 3, 2017

As the summer wraps up, it's time to get ready for school! Check to see if you have all these materials:

  1. At least 4-5 pencils
  2. 2-3 pens (Black, Blue, Red)
  3. Highlighters
  4. Notebooks
  5. Binders/Portfolio/Folders
